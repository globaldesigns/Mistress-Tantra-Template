Five Sacred Tantric Offerings

❦

Each offering has been carefully crafted through years of practice and devotion. They are not services — they are ceremonies. Each one meets you where you are and guides you to where your soul is calling.

Offering I

Tantric Ritual Session

❦

The foundational journey into the temple of your body. This is a sacred ceremony of breath, touch, and energetic awakening — designed for those who are ready to experience their sensuality as a spiritual practice for the first time, or who wish to return to the roots of tantric wisdom.

Through guided breathwork, conscious touch, and the ancient art of energetic circulation, you will discover that your body holds an infinite reservoir of sensation, pleasure, and healing. The ritual unfolds at its own pace — unhurried, intentional, and deeply reverent.

90 minutes · Introductory & All Levels

Offering II

Sacred Intimacy Session

❦

Deepen your capacity for connection — with yourself and with others. Sacred Intimacy is an exploration of the art of conscious relating through embodied practice, tender exploration, and the courage to be truly seen.

This offering goes beyond technique. It is about unraveling the stories you carry about your desires, your worthiness, and your capacity for love. Through guided practices in conscious communication, eye gazing, and heart-centered touch, you will learn to meet another soul from a place of radical presence rather than performance.

2 hours · Intermediate & Advanced

Offering III

Breathwork Healing Journey

❦

Release, heal, and expand through the power of guided breath. This is a journey inward — a descent through the layers of tension, story, and self-protection into the vast, luminous space that lives beneath it all.

Using a combination of circular breathing, breath retention, and tantric breath techniques, you will access altered states of consciousness, unlock dormant life force energy, and release emotional holdings that have been trapped in the body for years — sometimes decades.

No previous experience is necessary. The breath knows the way. You simply have to follow.

75 minutes · All Levels

Book This Offering

Offering IV

Couples Tantra Journey

❦

Rediscover the sacred in your partnership. The Couples Journey is a deeply intimate, guided experience designed for partners who wish to dissolve the walls that have built between them and return to the raw, electric connection that first brought them together.

Through tantric practices, conscious touch, and the art of energetic communion, you will learn to see each other again — not through the fog of routine and expectation, but through the eyes of wonder and desire. This is not couples therapy. This is a homecoming.

2.5 hours · Couples Only

Book This Offering

Offering V

Private Tantra Retreat

❦

The ultimate immersion. A half-day or full-day private retreat designed for those who are ready to go deep — past the surface, past the familiar, into the uncharted territories of their own sensual and spiritual potential.

This is a bespoke experience, carefully tailored to your unique intentions and desires. It may include any combination of tantric ritual, breathwork, sacred intimacy, meditation, somatic release, and guided self-inquiry. Every element is woven together into a seamless journey of transformation.

Held in a private sanctuary in San Francisco, with time for integration, reflection, and the gentle return to ordinary awareness. This is the deepest offering I provide — and the most profound.

4–8 hours · By Application Only
