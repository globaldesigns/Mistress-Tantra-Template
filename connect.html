The Sacred Path

How the Booking Process Unfolds

From your first inquiry to the moment you step through the temple door, every step of the process is held with intention, care, and reverence.

01

Step 1: Your Inquiry

You reach out — a message, a question, a whisper of readiness. There is no pressure and no expectation. I receive every inquiry with genuine warmth and curiosity about your unique journey.

02

Step 2: Initial Connection

We exchange a few messages or schedule a brief call — a moment to feel into whether we are the right match. This is a mutual discernment. Trust is the foundation of everything we will build together.

03

Step 3: Session Preparation

Once confirmed, I send you guidance for preparing — body, mind, and spirit. How to arrive, what to wear, what to release before you walk through the door. This preparation is part of the ritual itself.

04

Step 4: Your Session

You arrive. You breathe. You let go. From that first moment of meeting, you are held in a space of radical acceptance and sacred attention. The journey unfolds — and everything changes.

The Practice

Privacy & Boundaries

Absolutely and without exception. Confidentiality is not a policy — it is a sacred vow. Nothing that occurs within the temple is shared with anyone, ever. Your privacy is non-negotiable.

My offerings are rooted in the ancient traditions of Tantra — a ceremonial, spiritual, and somatic practice. Sessions may include breathwork, conscious touch, and energy work as part of a holistic healing experience. This is not a conventional hands-off coaching session, nor is it a conventional hands-on bodywork modality. It is its own sacred practice, and every session is guided by your boundaries and comfort.

Emotional release is a natural and welcome part of this work. Tears, laughter, trembling — all are honored here. You will never be rushed or judged. The space is held with deep compassion, and you remain in full control of your experience at every moment.
