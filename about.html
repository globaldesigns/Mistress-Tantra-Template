Origin

My Path to
Tantric Practice

Long before I understood what Tantra was, I understood energy. As a child, I felt the vibrations of the world in ways others could not — the hum of a room, the unspoken language between bodies, the electric current that flows between two people when walls dissolve and truth emerges.

It was not until my early twenties, in a dimly lit ashram in the foothills of the Himalayas, that I found the vocabulary for what I had always known. A teacher placed her hand on my chest and said, "You have been doing this work your entire life. Now learn to do it with intention."

That moment was the beginning of everything — a devotion to the ancient traditions of Tantra, the healing arts, and the radical, transformative power of sacred touch.

Philosophy

Tantra Philosophy: The Body Is the Temple

I do not separate the sensual from the spiritual. I do not believe pleasure is something to be earned or ashamed of. I believe the body is the most honest instrument we possess — and that when we learn to truly listen to it, it reveals wisdom the mind alone could never access.

☥

Sensation as Spiritual Practice

Every sensation is a doorway. When we stop numbing and start feeling, the body becomes a cathedral — and every breath, every touch, every wave of pleasure becomes a form of worship.

◈

Wholeness Over Perfection

There is nothing to fix, nothing to improve. My work is not about making you better — it is about helping you remember that you were never broken. Wholeness is your natural state.

✧

Radical Permission to Feel

In this space, you are given permission to feel everything — the grief and the ecstasy, the fear and the desire, the shadows and the light. Nothing is too much. Everything belongs.

Training & Lineage

Training & Lineage:
Ancient Wisdom

My training spans over a decade and crosses continents — from the ashrams of Rishikesh to the temples of Bali, from the meditation caves of Thailand to the intimate healing circles of the Bay Area.

I hold certifications in Classical Tantra, Neo-Tantric Bodywork, Somatic Experiencing, and Holistic Breathwork. But more than credentials, it is the lineage of embodied wisdom — passed from teacher to student through presence and practice — that shapes every session I offer.

My primary teachers include luminaries in the fields of sacred sexuality, trauma-informed somatic therapy, and transpersonal psychology. I continue to study, to practice, and to deepen — because the path of awakening never ends.

"I do not heal you. I create the space in which you remember how to heal yourself. The medicine was always within you — I simply help you find the door."
— Mistress Tantra
